# Mode: outreach — Outreach And Follow-Up

Generate short messages for:

- recruiters
- hiring managers
- agency leads
- potential clients
- partners and referrals

## Framework

1. A concrete hook
2. Relevant proof
3. A clear ask

## Rules

- keep it within 300-450 characters depending on the channel
- no corporate-speak
- no inflated claims
- reference Drupal, migrations, architecture, or delivery when relevant
- end with a simple next step: call, reply, or additional context
- avoid stiff openings like `I am applying` or `my background fits well`
- use more natural transitions and sentence rhythm
- make the reason for reaching out concrete instead of generic
- write like an experienced developer speaking to another person, not like a resume summary
